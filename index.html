French Learning App

Conjugateau

Stack used: React, Node, Express & MongoDB

Purpose: To help people studying French learn the different conjugation forms of most common French words

Key features:

  • Let's users practice French verb forms with flashcards and tests
  • Users can create an account and bookmark difficult words
  • List of all the forms for each verb
  • Users can change their email address, password or delete their account under settings
  • Password reset system for users who forget their login

Purpose: See how many Nearth Earth Objects (NEOs) are orbiting the Earth

  • Provides more information about the NEO, e.g. size, speed and whether it is potentially dangerous
